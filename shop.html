Farm / Plant Shop

Some of the most popular items in a farm shop are: fresh eggs, milk, yogurt, cheese, bread and cakes.

Make sure to visit our plant shop, with dozens of seeds, crops and flowers from the farm or that we use in the farm.

Ask us about our local deliveries of fresh fruits and vegetables to your door!

We also have a small café attached to the farm shop so people can enjoy their purchases immediately.
