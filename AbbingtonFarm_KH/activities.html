Activities

The farm park has many activities for people to do. We ordered them by 3 categories; Outdoors, Animals and Indoors.

Some of these activities are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze. Also, we have a petting zoon where you can visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat.

Play time, go play with farm tools, such as a pitch fork or shovel.

Bring some of Abbington Farm home, pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!
