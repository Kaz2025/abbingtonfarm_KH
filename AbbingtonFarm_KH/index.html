Welcome to Abbington Farm

Welcome to our farm shop website, where you can find all the information that you need about our products.

At Abbington Farm, we are passionate about providing our community with fresh, high-quality produce and products directly from our farm. Our journey began 7 years ago with a simple vision: to create a place where people can connect with nature, enjoy wholesome food, and experience the joys of farm life.

The farm shop has a wide range of fresh product and products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon. We have free-range eggs that are laid by the chickens on our farm. Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour. Choose from award winning pies with fillings such as: apricot or blackberry; hand-made chocolates; local honey and marmalade; homemade breads and cakes; locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers and ciders. We also sell fresh vegetables grown in our garden!

Abbington Farm's Story

About

At Abbington Farm, we are passionate about providing our community with fresh, high-quality produce and products directly from our farm. Our journey began 7 years ago with a simple vision: to create a place where people can connect with nature, enjoy wholesome food, and experience the joys of farm life.

Founded by Noel McLaunen, Abbington Farm started as a small family farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture and ethical farming practices. Over the years, we have grown into a thriving farm shop that offers a wide range of products, from fresh fruits and vegetables to homemade jams, baked goods, and artisanal crafts.

Learn more about our Missions and Values on our About page.
