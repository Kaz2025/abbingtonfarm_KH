Café

Welcome to Our Cozy Café

Nestled within the serene surroundings of Abbington Farm, our café offers a delightful escape where you can relax and enjoy the freshest farm-to-table fare. Whether you're looking for a hearty breakfast, a light lunch, or a sweet treat, our café has something for everyone.

Fresh and Local Ingredients

At our café, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients sourced directly from our farm and local producers. Our menu features seasonal dishes that highlight the best of what our farm has to offer, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavor and goodness.

Menu Highlights

- Breakfast: Start your day with our delicious homemade granola, farm-fresh eggs, and freshly baked pastries. - Lunch: Enjoy a variety of sandwiches, salads, and soups made with fresh, organic ingredients. - Desserts: Indulge in our selection of cakes, cookies, and pies, all made from scratch using traditional recipes. - Beverages: Savor our freshly brewed coffee, herbal teas, and refreshing juices.

Special Dietary Options

We cater to various dietary needs, offering gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Just let us know your preferences, and we'll be happy to accommodate you.

Cozy Atmosphere

Our café is designed to be a welcoming space where you can unwind and enjoy the beauty of the countryside. With comfortable seating, rustic décor, and a warm ambiance, it's the perfect spot to relax with family and friends.

Outdoor Seating

Take advantage of our outdoor seating area, where you can enjoy your meal while taking in the picturesque views of the farm. It's an ideal setting for a sunny day or a peaceful evening.

Events and Workshops

We regularly host events and workshops at our café, including cooking classes, farm tours, and seasonal celebrations. Check our Events page for upcoming activities and join us for a memorable experience.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit our café and experience the charm of Abbington Farm. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor, we look forward to welcoming you and sharing the bounty of our farm.

Dietary Requirements

Gluten-Free

Products:

Fresh fruits and vegetables, gluten-free bread, gluten-free baked goods, gluten-free pasta, and more.

Labeling:

All gluten-free products are clearly labeled for your convenience.

Note:

Our gluten-free products are prepared in a facility that also processes wheat. We take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but we cannot guarantee it.

Dairy-Free

Products:

Dairy-free milk alternatives (almond, soy, oat), dairy-free cheese, dairy-free yogurt, and more.

Labeling:

All dairy-free products are clearly labeled.

Note:

Our dairy-free products are suitable for those with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies.
