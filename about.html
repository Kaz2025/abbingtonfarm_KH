About Us

Welcome to Abbington Farm!

At Abbington Farm, we are passionate about providing our community with fresh, high-quality produce and products directly from our farm. Our journey began 7 years ago with a simple vision: to create a place where people can connect with nature, enjoy wholesome food, and experience the joys of farm life.

Our Story

Founded by Noel McLaunen, Abbington Farm started as a small family farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture and ethical farming practices. Over the years, we have grown into a thriving farm shop that offers a wide range of products, from fresh fruits and vegetables to homemade jams, baked goods, and artisanal crafts.

Our Missions

Our mission is to: Provide Fresh, Local Produce: We believe in the importance of eating fresh, locally-sourced food. Our farm produces a variety of seasonal fruits and vegetables, ensuring that you get the best quality produce all year round. Promote Sustainable Farming: We are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and promote biodiversity. Our methods include crop rotation, organic farming, and minimal use of pesticides. Support the Community: We strive to support our local community by collaborating with nearby farmers and artisans. By shopping with us, you are helping to sustain local businesses and preserve the rural way of life.

Our Values

Quality: We take pride in offering products that are fresh, nutritious, and of the highest quality. Sustainability: Our farming practices are designed to be environmentally friendly and sustainable. Community: We are dedicated to supporting and engaging with our local community. Transparency: We believe in being open and honest about our farming practices and the origins of our products.

Meet the team

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who share a love for farming and a commitment to excellence. From our farmers to our shop staff, everyone at Abbington Farm is dedicated to providing you with the best possible experience.
